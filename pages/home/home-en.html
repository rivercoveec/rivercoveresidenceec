Rivercove Residences EC Details

Rivercove Residences EC is a 99-years leasehold development located in Sengkang and Anchorvale area. It is a beautiful development with the full waterfront facing Sengkang Riverside Park. In additional, it has an unblock view making it one of the more unique ECs. Many of the units are also facing away from the LRT Station to reduce noise exposure. There are approximately 635 units that range from 3 to 5 bedrooms, whichever fits your needs.
